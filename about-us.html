About Us

Collins Structural Steel Fabrication in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada

Collins Industries Ltd. (CIL) is an Edmonton based structural steel fabrication company, established by Paul Collins in 1984 the organization is founded on ethical, responsible business practices. For over 25 years, CIL has served the commercial, industrial and institutional steel fabrication markets, while providing a corporate environment which encourages creativity and continuous improvement.

With increasing demand for value-added service, CIL is a leader in the steel fabrication industry in the delivery of Building Information Modeling (BIM) and design assistance through licenced third party engineers.

In support of these initiatives, CIL has invested heavily in the latest CNC equipment and 3D modeling software. The organization has developed and maintained a quality workforce that utilizes our structural steel fabrication technology to produce the highest quality workmanship at maximum efficiency. We have achieved this through our continuous investment in on-the-job training, mentoring, Alberta Apprenticeship Program training, and by supporting continuing education.

In addition, our detailed project and pre-production planning system enables CIL to offer one of the fastest turn-around times in the structural steel fabrication industry.

Collins Industries further demonstrates its commitment to the industry through our Canadian Welding Bureau certification, ISO 9001-2008 designation and Canadian Institute of Steel Construction (CISC) membership.

The foregoing all support our continuing focus on maintaining a leadership position as a quality and customer focused, cost effective structural steel fabricator.
