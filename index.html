About Us

Collins Structural Steel Fabrication in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada

Collins Industries Ltd. (CIL) is an Edmonton based structural steel fabrication company, established by Paul Collins in 1984 the organization is founded on ethical, responsible business practices. For over 25 years, CIL has served the commercial, industrial and institutional steel fabrication markets, while providing a corporate environment which encourages creativity and continuous improvement.
